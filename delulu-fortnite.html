About this software

Delulu is a weekends-only mode which can only be played with Proximity Voice Chat enabled, where companionship is assumed and betrayal is expected. Matches start with 80 real players that descend upon a random location on the playable Island. As everyone will be joining the match solo, they can either eliminate any other players they encounter or form a Squad with them. Up to 4 players can be in a Squad, although larger alliances can be formed through communication and teamwork. This mode's loot pool mostly consists of items that work well in teams as a way to encourage teamwork. To this effect, special Supply Crates and Locked Chests with high-tier loot spawn during gameplay, and they all require two or more players to open. Players can join and leave parties through the use of Emotes, which will not trigger any party notifications. Thus, nobody will know when you intend to betray your Squad. The last Squad standing at the end of the game will earn a Victory Royale.
